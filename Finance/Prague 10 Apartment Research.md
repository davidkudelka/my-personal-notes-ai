## Mortgage Calculation

### Current rates (March 2026)

### CNB rules (2026, owner-occupied)
- **LTV:** max 80% (i.e. minimum 20% down payment)
- **LTV under 36 years old:** up to 90% ✅ **— applies to you (10% down payment)**
- **DSTI:** max 45% of net monthly income toward all debt payments
- **DTI:** max 8.5× annual net income

### Your income for mortgage purposes

| Income source | Monthly (CZK) | Bank likelihood |
|--------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Base salary | 88,000 | ✅ Always counted |
| ESPP | 22,000 | ✅ Usually counted (shown on payslip) |
| RSU | ~33,333 avg | ⚠️ Depends on bank — employer confirmation needed |
| Extra salary | ~21,667 avg | ⚠️ Counted at 50–100% if 2+ years documented |
| **Conservative total** | **110,000** | Base + ESPP only |
| **Optimistic total** | **165,000** | All income streams |

### Maximum mortgage you qualify for

**At conservative income (110,000 CZK/month net):**
- DSTI limit (45%): max monthly payment = 49,500 CZK
- At 4.9%, 30 years → max mortgage ≈ **9,300,000 CZK**
- DTI limit (8.5×): 8.5 × 1,320,000 = **11,220,000 CZK**
- → **Effectively capped at ~9.3M by DSTI**

**At full income (165,000 CZK/month net):**
- Max mortgage: **14,000,000+ CZK**

*For a 5M apartment you need ~4M mortgage — well within reach either way.*

## Key Considerations

### Timing
- Mortgage rates trending upward in 2026 (+0.4% expected) → the sooner you lock in, the better
- Prague property prices up ~14% YoY — waiting costs money on both sides (higher price + higher rate)

### Mortgage strategy
- **3-year fixed** currently offers best rates (4.39–4.59%) — good balance of low rate + ability to refinance
- Get competing offers from at least 3 banks — competition is the strongest negotiation lever
- Banks to compare: ČSOB, Česká spořitelna, Komerční banka, Raiffeisenbank, mBank

### Property checklist (before buying)
- [ ] OV (osobní vlastnictví) confirmed — not cooperative
- [ ] No legal encumbrances on the property (check katastru nemovitostí)
- [ ] Building energy performance certificate (PENB)
- [ ] HOA fees (správa domu) — ask for last 2 years of meeting minutes
- [ ] Planned special levies or major renovations in the building
- [ ] Age and condition of wiring, plumbing, windows
- [ ] Cellar / parking / balcony included?

### Tax
- No property transfer tax for buyers in CZ (abolished 2020) ✅
- Property tax (daň z nemovitosti) — small annual fee, typically a few thousand CZK/year
- Mortgage interest is NOT tax-deductible in CZ (abolished 2021)
