    * **Writing is a way to organise your brain**
      * Writing something down from you own memory helps you with remembering everything. It helps your brain to organise everything
    * **Writing is a way to learn**
      * If you get into active motion of writing it let's you experiment, you will learn much more than by just reading
    * **Writing helps you identify mistakes**
      * It helps you identify mistakes in your own thinking. It will let you look at the problem from different perspective
    * ### **How to write more?**
      * **Design docs** - pay more attention to them, always try to improve something
      * **Answering on stack overflow** - start with short contextual answers on stack overflow
      * **Blog** - start to write about something on your own blog post
