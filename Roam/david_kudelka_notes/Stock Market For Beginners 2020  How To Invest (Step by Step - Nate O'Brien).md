      * The Inteligent Investor - #book-mention
      * **Active investor** - at least once a week you looking over the stocks you own, you are looking at the news, making sure everything is going smoothly (5-10 hours a week at least)
      * **Passive investor** - dollar cost averaging, ETFs or mutual funds
    * **Short Term vs Long Term**

    * **Stocks vs Funds**
      * With stocks you own piece of company
      * With ETFs you own piece of all companies
