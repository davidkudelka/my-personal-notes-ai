    * Questions
      * Task delegation
        * How to manage your works with planning and giving tasks or support to your team members?
          * Your team always goes first before your own tasks
        * Effective delegation of tasks. How to do it?
        * The best thing you can do as an impactful leader is to unlock possibilities so your team can work effectively **without you**. How to work on this?
          * How to test this? If your processes are in place, take 2 weeks vacation without access to your email and see where you are still blocking your team
        * How to manage team members so they have personal development roadmap and they are still happy and that they are good team players
        * How to effectively refine tasks, break down stories?
      * Self-development
        * How to broaden your perspective and shape long term goals for your technology stack?
        * How to include your team to shape and actively create those tech decisions?
          * It matters a lot, because all of you have different perspectives
      * Technical leadership of the project
        * How to implement improvements to the project (time to research and how to sell those)
          * Estimation of tasks, how to take into consideration who is working on the ticket?
          * How to better understand product?
            * What to do when goals from business are not clear?
      * Team management
        * How to manage team properly (how many 1on1's and how much time to spend with other team members so I can still focus on my own work)?
