    * The secret to being 10x times more productive is mastery of **abstraction**
    * **Abstraction is the Key to Simple Code**
      * "Simplicity is about substracting the obvious and adding the meaningful" - [[John Maeda]]
      * 2 main concepts
        * **Generalization** - Removing the repeated parts (the obvious) and hiding them behind an abstraction
        * **Specialization** - Applying the abstraction for particular use-case, adding just what needs to be different (the meaningful)
      * The right abstractions are powerful levers that can impact productivity dramatically. Abstraction is not a dirty word. Modules, functions, variables, classes - all of these are forms of abstraction and the entire reason any of them exist is to make abstraction and composition of abstraction easier
      * Map is good example of abstraction
