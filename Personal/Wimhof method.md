  * **Process**
    * **Breathing**
      * 1. Sit down, get to comfortable position, long breaths to the stomach
      * 2. Breath in as much as possible, when breathing out do push it, just let it go automatically, repeat until your head starts to be dizzy
      * 3. Breathe out and hold your breath as long as possible - **note down your time how long you can hold your breath**
      * 4. When you need to breath again, breathe in and hold your breath for 15 seconds
      * 5. Breathe out and repeat the process as you need. It's good to start with 3 repeats and eventually get to 7
    * **Stretching**
      * After breathing do little stretching and maybe Yoga or push ups **note down how many push ups you can do**. This process should take just few minutes
    * **Setting up your mind**
      * Try to imagine fire inside your body, or anything that brings warm into your body
    * **Cold bath**
      * Get into cold bath
      * **Track how long you can actually stay in the cold bath**
