  * Notes:
    * **1. Stop going to bed at inconsistent times** - inconsistent amount of sleep is generally bad for your memory and energy levels during the day
    * **2. Stop doing your main activities before your bed time** - set wind down time before you want to fall asleep. Let's say setting 30' mins time before you want to fall asleep, brush your teeth and start doing something relaxing, maybe read a book or do something that is easy to put down
    * **3. Stop using the internet before bed** - internet is very hard to put down, when your wind down times rolls down, put down all devices
    * **4. Stop drinking caffeinated drinks before bed** - it damages your sleep quality
    * **5. Stop going to bed angry** - 
    * **6. Stop working out late at night** - it raises your body temperature and it takes some time for your body to cool down
    * **7. Stop Leaving messes for your future self** - when you wake up, you are immedietelly interupting momentum with cleaning stuff
