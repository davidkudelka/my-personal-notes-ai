    * ### Storytelling: How to Begin
      * Presenting is like a journey, you always want to get people somewhere
      * You have to start with end goal
      * Audience then understands sense of progress
      * You have to practice your story, why should people listen to you, what is the point of this ? Practice your story everywhere you go
      * **Best thing to do is to come up with idea with spirit of giving**
        * It shouldn't be about you selling things
        * When someone shows up idea to give something, new idea of giving something
        * Showing with desire to give is the best way how to do that
      * The very best way how to start presentation is not to start with you reference (proving that you have the right to tell them something), it is not even facts and figures (those can come later on), but to start with **a STORY**
    * ### Design Your Talk
      * They way you can start is to write an outline
      * Most of the time you are trying to build an argument and then in the end you reveal conclusion with should follow with TADA
      * Actually the best way how to do this is exactly the opposite
      * What really matters when you are presenting something
        * Not to really understand the problem from technical stand point, but to understand the human in the problem, persons that should benefit from the technical solution
    * ### Presenting: Bring It All Together
      * 1. Start with end in mind
      * 2. Choose the story you want to start with
      * 3. Making outline, write an outline as you imagine and try to reverse it
      * 4. Practice out loud, Repeat
      * 5. Prepare to present - the words and content is just half of the work
      * 6. Present, record, upload
    * ### Tips for Presenting
      * Be yourself
        * Bring your own personality, dress the way it feels comfortable to you
      * Show up to give
        * You are offering them a new way of looking at things, something they never heard before, you are there to educate, to help
      * Own the room
        * You are the one on the stage, you should remember to use it
      * Go quiet
        * Instead of saying ehm... just be quiet, the best way how to get out of lost thought is to be yourself
      * This is exciting!
        * Re-interpret fear of presenting into excitement, you are going to stage, nervous, you stop and tell yourself this is exciting
      * Slow down
      * Talk to one person
        * Looking at individual, looking into faces, just imagine telling it to this one person
      * Accept the applause
        * It is like gift
      * You don't have to know all the answers
      * Know your stuff
      * Keep the words off the slides
