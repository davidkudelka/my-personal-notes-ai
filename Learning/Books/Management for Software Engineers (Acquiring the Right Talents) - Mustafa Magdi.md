  * Notes:
    * ## Leader vs Manager
      * A manager sets targets, plan work, organise, delegate tasks, and coordinate. A leader is able to create an environment where each individual feels totally committed to doing a great job
      * Management is more quantitive, while leadership is more qualitative
    * ## Acquiring the Right Talent
      * You need to hire according to your values, if ownership is one of your values in the interview process you should be looking for people with this value - they will form your company going forward
      * Any extra skills is good one and you should be aware of those skills people you hire have
      * ### Team retention
        * Build team skills and track this among all your team members, try to always expand their knowledge in certain areas
        * Recognise and reward - this will win their loyalty
        * "People leave managers, not companies" - this is very correct based on most of the cases I have seen during my career
      * ### Defining the Requirements
        * You have to define why are you hiring
        * Skill requirement area
          * Technical
          * Communication
          * Leadership
        * You have to align with the team that this candidate is assigned to
      * ### Preparing and Designing your Questions List
        * You have to have a written test, like a list of questions
        * You prepare the communication and leadership questions
        * The important question, do they have potential, motivation, and attitude?
        * Also, you might think about critical thinking skills
